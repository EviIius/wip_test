Central Analytics Support & Enablement (CASE) is our enterprise-level support platform, serving over 20,000 data scientists, data analysts, MLOps engineers, BI developers, and other stakeholders across the bank.

CASE offers a comprehensive suite of features, including “CASEY,” a chatbot microservice built on Microsoft Copilot Studio, designed to utilize intent-driven, non-General AI/LLM capabilities. Furthermore, the platform incorporates the Collective Intelligence (CI) framework, a conditional ticketing system, and MS Graph-enabled search capabilities.

These features enable product and support teams to effectively address customer needs, manage product knowledge documentation, and handle product inquiries within a unified platform, thereby enhancing collaboration and efficiency across the organization.
